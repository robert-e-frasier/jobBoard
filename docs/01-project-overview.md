# 01 - Project Overview

## What is the project?

This project is a job board designed to connect recruiters with potential applicants in a more transparent and efficient way than many existing platforms.

## Why are you building it?

During my own job searches, I noticed several recurring issues that affect both recruiters and job seekers. Recruiters often receive hundreds of applications from candidates who do not meet the qualifications for the position, making the hiring process time-consuming and inefficient.

On the other side, many job postings contain inaccurate or misleading information. On of the most common examples is positions being labeled as "Remote" when they are actually hybrid roals, forcing applicants to spend additional time researching opportunities that do not match their needs. Many existing job boards do not provide recruiters with clear enough options to accurately represent the type of position they are advertising.

This project aims to address these problems by providing a platform that encourages more accurate job listings and better matches between employers and candidates.

## Target Audience

* Recruiters and hiring managers seeking qualified candidates.
* Individuals searching for employment opportunities.

## Goals

The primary goal of this project is to simplify the hiring process for both recruiters and job seekers by improving the accuracy of job listings, reducing mismatched applications, and providing a cleaner, more transparent experience for everyone involved.
